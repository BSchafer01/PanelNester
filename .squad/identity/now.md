# What We're Focused On

The current active slice is a UI cleanup pass on the Material and Results pages. The team is removing leftover validation-heavy controls and summaries from production views, and reworking the Results page into a cleaner operator workspace with tabbed details on the left and a resizable sheet viewer on the right.

This work should preserve the established dark theme, Three.js-based viewer flow, PDF export path, and material management behaviors that still matter to end users while reducing clutter.
